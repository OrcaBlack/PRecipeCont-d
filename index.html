PANCAKE RECIPE

A pancake, also known as a hotcake, griddlecake, or flapjack, is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk, and butter, and then cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan. It is a type of batter bread. Pancakes may be served at any time of the day or year with a variety of toppings or fillings, but they have developed associations with particular times and toppings in different regions. Provided below is a recipe for making the best homemade pancakes using simple ingredients which are readly avaliable in your kitchen. Let's get started.

Ingredients List

  • Flour: We use all-purpose flour for these pancakes, however other types of flour can be used based on preference
  • Baking powder: Addition of baking powder keeps the pancakes light and fluffy. Baking soda can be used as a substitute
  • Sugar: Add just enough sugar to the batter to ensure a great taste
  • Salt: A pinch of salt is added to the batter to enchance the overall taste
  • Milk: The milk could be either diary or non-diary as both work for this recipe
  • Butter: Adds up to the richness of the pancakes, omit salt if the butter being used is salted
  • Eggs: Helps bind batter together, adding up to moisture of the pancake
  • Flavor (Nutmerg): Enhances the flavor as well as taste of pancakes

How to Make Pancakes From Scratch

  1. Add flour, baking powder and salt in a large bowl then whisk
  2. Whisk butter, milk, egg, and nutmerg in another bowl
  3. Stir the wet ingredients into the flour. As you stir, the batter will start to thicken and get bubbly so avoid mixing too much to keep pancakes fluffy
  4. Heat frying pan to medium heat and add butter. The butter give pancakes amazing crispy edges
  5. Flip the pancakes when the edges look dry and bubbles appear and pop on the surfaces
  6. Cook each side for about 2 minutes. Reduce cooking heat if you notice them browning too quickly
